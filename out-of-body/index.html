Out of Body / Out of Mind

Visual Identity & Packaging
Apr 2026 · Group Project w/ Francisca Baptista & João Jorge
Visual Identity Packaging Editorial

A special edition double-vinyl compilation exploring the emotional arc of resistance in the modern age. Structured in two parts, it moves from collective protest to internal, psychological resistance.

My contribution: Concept development, full visual identity system, and design of physical outputs: dual-cover gatefold LP (covers, interior, sleeves, labels), CDs (cover and discs), box set & interior, and zine & poster.
