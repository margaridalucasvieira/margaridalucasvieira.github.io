Project Details

Slanted by Art(ifice) — Editorial Design

Pair Project w/ Francisca Baptista · DC III · FBAUL · Nov 2025

Art(ifice) is a monthly publication exploring design as the product of the interplay between the designer and the design. Each issue features one designer, examining their work, identity, and ideological influences.

Issue 5 — Slanted takes on Peter Saville's manifesto "Be careful what you wish for," delving into his background, inspirations, and iconic work — from Joy Division's Unknown Pleasures through his later career.

103 pages · 18×30 cm · 4 rows, 8 col. grid · Gutter: 8mm · Baseline: 14pt
Paper: Rives 110g · Language: English

Out of Body / Out of Mind — Visual Identity & Packaging

Group Project · DC III · FBAUL · Apr 2026

A special edition double-vinyl compilation exploring the emotional arc of resistance in the modern age. Structured in two parts, it moves from collective protest to internal, psychological resistance.

My contribution: Concept development, full visual identity system, and design of physical outputs:

  • Dual-cover gatefold LP (covers, interior, sleeves, labels)
  • CDs (cover and discs)
  • Box set & interior
  • Zine & poster

Collaborators: Francisca Baptista (website & poster), João Jorge (mockups & video)

Cactus Quo — Brand Identity Design

Solo & Group Project w/ Francisca Baptista, Leonor Bastos & Matilde Carvalho · Jun 2025

Started with 10 statements, ended with a community. Cactus Quo is a feminist activist collective with a purpose: to spread awareness and call for action.

We spread our message through: quote posters, interactive posters, logo stickers, QR code stickers, Google Forms, Instagram page with posts and reels. My brand identity design was the chosen one.

nocturna — Interaction Design

Solo Project · DC IV · FBAUL · Nov 2025 · Language: Portuguese

Meet nocturna, an empathetic AI assistant designed for home environments. It helps users improve their sleep quality and nighttime routine by easing restlessness and transforming their space into a calming sanctuary.

Full UX/UI case study of a product fully conceptualized, analyzed, and developed by me — including ethnographic research, double diamond methodology, sketching, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes.

UX Research Wireframes Prototyping AI/ML

AFFECTCHA — Speculative Interaction Design

Group Project · DC IV · FBAUL · 2026 · → Ongoing

AFFECTCHA is a speculative interaction design project that examines how to verify human presence in a future where AI can convincingly simulate emotion and memory. Addressing the "affect authentication" problem.

Phases: Problem statement, research, goals & hypothesis → Quiz, personas, user journeys, wireframes → TBC.
