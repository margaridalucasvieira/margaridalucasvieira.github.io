Slanted by Art(ifice)

Editorial Design
Nov 2025 · Pair Project w/ Francisca Baptista
Editorial Design Pair Project Peter Saville

Art(ifice) is a monthly publication exploring design as the product of the interplay between the designer and the design. Each issue features one designer, examining their work, identity, and ideological influences.

Issue 5 — Slanted takes on Peter Saville's manifesto "Be careful what you wish for," delving into his background, inspirations, and iconic work — from Joy Division's Unknown Pleasures through his later career.
